FAQs

What is Virtuosi?

Virtuosi is a music community offering workshops, membership benefits, and exclusive merchandise to help music enthusiasts grow and connect.

How do I join Virtuosi?

You can join Virtuosi by visiting our Membership page and signing up for the plan that suits you best.

Are the workshops beginner-friendly?

Yes! Our workshops are designed for all skill levels, including beginners, and include guidance from expert instructors.

Can I purchase merch without being a member?

Yes, our merch is available to everyone. Visit our Merch page to explore the collection.

Do you offer refunds for workshops?

Refunds are available up to 7 days before the workshop date. Please contact us for assistance.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through the Contact page or email us at support@virtuosi.com.
